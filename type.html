Type Task:

Axial: The main premiss of this typography style is that the text follows along an imaginary line, the texts only strict rule being to follow. This means that the text can be aligned either side and even that the line does not have to be vertical. The way I achieved this is through creating multiple diagonal lines and arranging them into a zigzag pattern, my aim to ultimately make a possibly boring or plain looking typographic system into something more unique.
